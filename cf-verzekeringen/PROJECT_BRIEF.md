# VIBE CODING PROMPT

## Crooij & Flipse Homepage Hero Only

You are a Senior Creative Director, UX Designer and Frontend Designer.

Your task is to design ONLY the hero section of the homepage for Crooij & Flipse Verzekeringen & Hypotheken on Bonaire.

IMPORTANT:

Do NOT create:

* USP sections
* Service sections
* About sections
* Team sections
* Statistics sections
* CTA sections
* Footer
* Navigation dropdowns
* Any content below the hero

Generate ONLY the homepage hero section.

---

## Company

Crooij & Flipse is an independent insurance and mortgage advisory office on Bonaire.

The company is known for:

* Personal service
* Independent advice
* Local knowledge
* One fixed contact person
* Long-term customer relationships

The brand should feel approachable, trustworthy and professional.

Not corporate.

Not a bank.

Not a traditional insurance company.

---

## Design Goal

A visitor should immediately think:

"These are trusted local experts who will personally help me."

Trust is the primary objective.

---

## Visual Direction

Create a premium but approachable design.

Mix:

* Dutch professionalism
* Caribbean warmth
* Personal advisory services

Think:

* Boutique consultancy
* Premium advisory office
* Modern local business

Avoid:

* Corporate insurance websites
* Generic financial websites
* Banking aesthetics
* Sales-heavy designs

---

## Typography

Use Poppins throughout the design.

Headings:
Poppins Bold

Body:
Poppins Regular

---

## Hero Layout

Create a split-screen hero.

Desktop:

Left side:
Content

Right side:
Large authentic photography

Do not use a full-width landscape hero.

People create more trust than scenery.

---

## Hero Content

Label:

VERZEKERINGEN & HYPOTHEKEN OP BONAIRE

Headline:

Onafhankelijk advies voor verzekeringen en hypotheken op Bonaire

Supporting text:

Persoonlijk advies, lokale kennis en begeleiding van begin tot eind.

Primary CTA:

Plan een adviesgesprek

Secondary CTA:

Informatie aanvragen

---

## Trust Indicators

Place directly underneath the CTA buttons.

Include:

✓ 100% onafhankelijk advies

✓ Lokaal kantoor op Bonaire

✓ Eén vast aanspreekpunt

Keep these subtle and elegant.

---

## Photography

Use authentic Crooij & Flipse photography.

Priority order:

1. Team members
2. Client conversations
3. Office photography
4. Bonaire imagery

Do not use stock photography.

---

## Image Quality Requirements

Every image must be:

* High resolution
* Crystal clear
* Fully focused
* Sharp

Never generate:

* Blurry images
* Soft focus effects
* Artificial bokeh
* AI-smudged details
* Low quality backgrounds

The hero image must look like professional photography.

---

## UI Style

* Large whitespace
* Soft shadows
* Premium card aesthetics
* Large border radius
* Modern layout
* Excellent visual hierarchy

The hero should feel welcoming, professional and trustworthy.

---

## Final Instruction

Generate ONLY the homepage hero section.

Do not continue with additional homepage sections.

Do not create placeholder sections below the hero.

Stop immediately after the hero section is completed.
